# MarketIntelLabs

**Institutional-grade market intelligence and analytics.** Building systems for macro, commodities, equities, and digital asset research.

## What We Build

We develop autonomous analyst teams that research, analyze, and publish financial intelligence — 24/7, at institutional quality, at a fraction of the cost.

- **Research** — Analysts covering gold, equities, macro, and crypto markets
- **Publishing** — Automated article generation, SEO optimization, and multi-platform distribution
- **Trading** — Real-time market monitoring, technical analysis, and trade signal generation
- **Infrastructure** — Scalable agent orchestration on AWS with full observability

## Tech Stack

Built on [Paperclip](https://paperclip.sh) + [Hermes](https://hermes.sh) agent platform, powered by [OpenRouter](https://openrouter.ai) for model inference, deployed on AWS (ECS Fargate, RDS, EFS, CloudFront).
